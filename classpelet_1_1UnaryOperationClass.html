Detailed Description

Holds a unary operation in the form

<OP> operand or operand <OP>

where <OP> is "++" (postfix), "++" (prefix), "--" (postfix), "--" (prefix), "+", "-", "!", "~", "(int)", "(double)", "(float)", "(string)", "(array)", "(object)", "(bool)", "(boolean)", "(unset)", "exit", "@"

The operand is stored in the Operand member
